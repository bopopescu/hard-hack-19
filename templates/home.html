VINE ASSIST

About

By Daniel Truong, Joachim Do, Casey Price, Angela Liu, William Martino

Depression is an extremely common disorder. To combat this, we have created a device that captures people's moods and displays a high-quality vine to cheer them up when they are sad. This can help whether or not someone actually has depression and would just help them feel better. However, this device is not only for the depressed, but for the mentally sound as well. This device will detect your mood, and play the appropriate vine. With the Vines Assist, we hope to aid in humanity's battle against mental health disorders.
